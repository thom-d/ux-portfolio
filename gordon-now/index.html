Gordon Now

Role: UI Designer | Gordon Food Service

Branding and visual design for the Gordon Now product landing page.
2017 - 2018.

What is Gordon Now, and what does it have to do with Google Home?

Gordon Now, is a digital assistant that has been layered on top of, and integrated with the Google Home technology. Its purpose is to support a B2B customer, with enhanced business functionality tailored to your operational needs.

Timeline

Earlier in spring 2017, a concept landing page had been created as part of a proposal for the Gordon Now digital assistant endeavor. In fall 2017, the project was given the greenlight with a very small timeline. As part of that, I was tasked with designing a new, updated landing page that would be quickly spun up in the Wordpress environment. My delivery timeline? About a day and a half.

With such a short timeline, I immediately began to conduct some inspiration research online. I knew I wanted to take the existing GFS brand aesthetic, and elevate and refine it for this landing page. The project and product are exciting. The promotional page should match. That meant a clear focus on the product, balancing clean typography and layout with bold (and darker) colors than normal. This would be a relatively new aesthetic for Gordon Food Service.

While visuals are a language and vehicle, content is the substance. Working with our Marketing content team, I was able to design a flow and craft a layout around the content and what we wanted to say.

The Goal: Rapidly stand up a new product landing page that was scalable for future features and additions such as - blogging, callouts for new Charlie features, a Get Started guide, and more.
