Supplier Central

Role: UX Researcher, UX and UI Designer | Gordon Food Service

Supplier Central, is a Gordon Food Service solution, created to unite resources in one place for vendor partners. I worked closely with development, our business stakeholder, project manager and product owner on a weekly sprint cadence.
2017.

Research and Competitive Analysis

The preparation timeline before development was fairly short. I made the decision to focus on understanding the business requirements, and performing a competitive analysis. I reviewed companies such as Walmart, Amazon, Sears and Zulily. I also developed a user flow to guide the application journey and various scenarios.

What I discovered, is that many vendor portal solutions are either overly complex or lacking in functionality.

Key Approach: Create a platform that was simple and easy to access.

Prototyping and User Testing

Working with the business, I was able to secure two vendor partners to test with. For this, I created an InVision prototype for the users to click through. I observed and noted any questions, hesitations other behaviors. After the observation, I was able to interview our users for more feedback.

Key Discovery: The platform was easy to use, but some of the labels and functionality needed more clarity.

UI Design, Production and Follow Up

Supplier Central today, features a central dashboard where SaaS solutions will soon be available. It also houses essential documents for vendor engagement.
At the request of the business, a follow up user feedback forum was conducted. Unfortunately, there was not enough functionality in production to yield strong feedback.

Future State: More functionality may be added to Supplier Central, but that is currently under review.
